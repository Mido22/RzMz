Methodology

Process

At Razzmatazz Foods we follow the basic Indian cooking procedures; consciously we have avoided any short cuts. Dal makhani is cooked overnight on slow tandoor fire; eggplants are roasted over charcoal to give that ethnic smoky flavor. All curries are cooked in braising pans; use of steam kettles is limited to only boiling.

Technology

Packing of products is done by advanced machines untouched by human hand. We use virgin food grade packing material. Products are flash frozen foods in ultra modern machines to maintain the flavor and freshness. Once frozen to -20° centigrade the products can be transported and delivered to various locations across the globe. We have cold storage facilities at our location to store frozen ready products. Shipments are sent to our clients in reefer containers to maintain -20° centigrade temperature. The time taken to complete the whole process is kept at bare minimum. The whole process from purchase to dispatch is religiously monitored and documented. This enables us to create a comprehensive traceability in the system.

Research & Development

Razzmatazz foods have an excellent R & D facility, equipped with most modern equipments. Our Chef’s and sensory team combine their years of experience to prepare and dish out the most delectable products. We also offer our expertise and services to our clients to improve their products. We assist our clients to develop and finalize recipes, package designing and photography. Beside product development we also offer services in market research, pilot studies and packaging design and development.
